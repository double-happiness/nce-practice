翻译: The gate of the residential area is locked at ten p.m.
疑问: Is the gate of the residential area locked at ten p.m.?
否定: The gate of the residential area is not locked at ten p.m.
提问(对「at ten p.m.」提问，问"几点锁"): What time is the gate of the residential area locked?
解析: residential area 小区；物业门禁。

# book=1 lesson=21 grammar=一般现在时,频率副词
中文: 物业每周二收垃圾。
翻译: The property management collects rubbish every Tuesday.
疑问: Does the property management collect rubbish every Tuesday?
否定: The property management does not collect rubbish every Tuesday.
提问(对「every Tuesday」提问，问"什么时候收"): When does the property management collect rubbish?
解析: property management 物业；收垃圾日程。

# book=2 lesson=35 grammar=间接引语

# book=2 lesson=37 grammar=现在完成进行时
中文: 楼上一直在装修，很吵。
翻译: They have been renovating upstairs, and it is very noisy.
疑问: Have they been renovating upstairs, and is it very noisy?
否定: They have not been renovating upstairs, so it is not noisy.
提问(对「upstairs」提问，问"在哪里装修"): Where have they been renovating?
解析: renovate 装修；邻里噪音抱怨。
